I am a creative ambivert with the tendency to allow my hyper-focus to morph into obsession. I am a writer, a dreamer, and a visual and auditory artist. I grew up writing songs and playing instruments, and as of 2020, I'm channeling my creative flow into the world of tech.

I lose hours designing web apps that are increasingly user-friendly and interesting to traverse. I live for collaboration and love constructive feedback and groupthink. I like working to understand the minds of those around me and they way they structure their reality (and their code) in unique ways. Worlds created by others are fascinating to me; I started reading at the age of 3 and will inhale novels in the course of a few days.

I also produce & direct songs, albums, and music videos for myself and for others. I began producing sound packs for global distribution in 2020, and am currently collaborating with three labels on three separate bodies of work: TextMe Records (California // SF based, currently working my artist project releases), Black Octopus (Canadian-European, currently releasing my sound packs and sound pack albums), and Ultra Music (Sony Subsidiary, my publisher

My tech journey is in it's earliest phases, where the coccoon has just broken open. My goal is to attain as much knowledge as possible; my dream is to work in biotech, so that I might help improve the health and general wellbeing and longevity of our species.

My favorite question to ask people is: if you had the choice, would you choose to live forever?
