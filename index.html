Who Is She

I am a creative ambivert with the tendency to allow my hyper-focus to morph into obsession. I am a writer, a dreamer, and a visual and auditory artist. I grew up writing songs and playing instruments, and as of 2020, I am channeling my creative flow into the world of tech.

  • JavaScript
  • React
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JQuery
  • Accessibility
  • Responsive Design
  • REST APIs
  • Firebase

I lose hours designing web apps that are increasingly user-friendly and interesting to traverse. I live for collaboration and love constructive feedback and groupthink. I love working to understand the minds of those around me. Other's unvierses are fascinating to me; I started reading at the age of 3 and will inhale novels in the course of a few days.

I also produce & direct songs, albums, and music videos for myself and others. I produce sound packs for global distribution, and am currently collaborating with three labels: TextMe Records (California // SF based, currently working my artist project releases), Black Octopus (Canadian-European, currently releasing my sound packs and sound pack albums), and Ultra Music (Sony Subsidiary, my publisher).

My tech journey is in it's earliest phases, where the coccoon has just broken open. My goal is to know as much as possible; my dream is to work in biotech, so that I might help improve the health and wellbeing of our species.

So tell me, if you had the choice, would you choose to live forever?
