Who Is She

I am a creative ambivert with the tendency to allow my hyper-focus to morph into obsession. I am a writer, a dreamer, and a visual and auditory artist. I grew up writing songs and playing instruments, and as of 2020, I am channeling my creative flow into the world of tech.

  1. JavaScript
  2. React
  3. HTML5
  4. CSS3
  5. JQuery
  6. Accessibility
  8. Responsive Design
  9. REST APIs
  10. Firebase

I also produce & direct songs, albums, and music videos for myself and others. I produce sound packs for global distribution. I lose hours designing web apps that are increasingly user-friendly and interesting to traverse.

I live for collaboration and love constructive feedback and groupthink. I love working to understand the minds of those around me. Other's unvierses are fascinating to me; I started reading at the age of 3 and will inhale novels in the course of a few days.

My tech journey is in it's earliest phases, where the coccoon has just broken open. My goal is to know as much as possible; my dream is to work in biotech, so that I might help improve the health and wellbeing of our species.

So tell me, if you had the choice, would you choose to live forever?
